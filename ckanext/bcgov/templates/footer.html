About Tracking

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. ("Google").

Google Analytics uses "cookies," which are text files stored on your computer that enable an analysis of your use of the website. Some of the information generated by the cookie about your use of this website may be transmitted to and stored on Google servers outside of Canada. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings in your browser; however, please note that if you do so, you may not be able to use the full functionality of this website. Furthermore, you can prevent Google’s collection and use of data generated by the cookie and related to your use of the website by downloading and installing the browser plug-in available at (http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en).

This website also specifies that Google Analytics apply IP Address Anonymization https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/2763052?hl=en so that complete IP addresses are never stored by Google.

You can learn more about Google’s Privacy Policies as they pertain to our partner use from http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/.
